Research

Research Interests

  • Intelligent system security, machine learning/deep learning model security, including recommender systems, social networks, computer vision, natural language processing, time series, knowledge graphs, autonomous driving systems, distributed systems, mobile intelligent terminals and other applications.
  • I am also part of the System Software and Security Lab at Fudan University.
